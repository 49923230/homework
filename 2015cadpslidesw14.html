Steps of assembly

1. Insert the component "base" and fix it in the working page.

2. Insert "adjuster base, hinge and cylinder".

3. Using "cylindrical mate" to mate the hole on each component.

4. Using "planar mate" to mate the combined face on each component.

5. Insert "adjuster and piston."

6. Using "slider mate" to mate "adjuster and piston".

7. Insert "connection and handle" and assemble them.

8. Give the limitation on "adjuster and piston" sliding part.
